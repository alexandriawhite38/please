Catch flights not feelings

Italy

I took this trip with my best friend and here is what we learned:

  • Understand the bus schedule and plan your route ahead of time.
  • wear comfortable shoes. you'll be walking A LOT!
  • Don't be afraid to take spontaneous detours.

California

5 of my best friend took this trip with me and here is what we learned:

  • Ask local where thier favorite places are.
  • Their is always free parking just ask around.
  • Get gelato.

Quebec City

My Family and I took this trip this winter and here is what we learned:

  • Everything is cheaper if paying in dollars so go shopping!
  • Get the choclate dipped ice cream.
  • Learn a little bit of french before going.
